Manufacturing & quality assurance

This overview explains how we think about traceability and inspection - it is not a substitute for a facility audit or third-party surveillance report.

Workflow (generic)

  1. Incoming steel inspection and release
  2. Forming / welding hold points per WPS
  3. Surface preparation and coating application under qualified procedures
  4. Holiday detection, thickness checks, and peel/adhesion tests as specified
  5. Final documentation bundle for release

ISO 9001 / ISO 14001 / ISO 45001 families are referenced on Baike as part of the management system posture - display official certificates only after client approval.
