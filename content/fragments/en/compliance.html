Quality management system

Hebei Hengyuan Industrial Co., Ltd strictly implements the ISO 9001:2015 quality management system, establishing a comprehensive quality control system from raw material procurement to manufacturing, quality testing, and warehousing. Our quality control department operates independently, equipped with professional technical personnel and advanced testing equipment to ensure every batch of products meets national standards and industry specifications.

Our quality control system mainly includes:

  • Raw material quality control — strict inspection of all incoming materials to ensure they meet design requirements, with a qualified supplier management system.
  • Production process control — checkpoints at key processes throughout production, with real-time monitoring of manufacturing parameters for consistent quality.
  • Finished product inspection — comprehensive inspection of each batch, including appearance, dimensions, coating thickness, adhesion, mechanical properties, and more.
  • Quality tracking system — product batch management and traceability so each batch can be traced back to raw material lots and production processes.

Quality policy

We adhere to the quality policy of "Quality First, Customer First, Continuous Innovation, Pursuit of Excellence" to provide customers with high-quality, reliable pipeline products and professional technical services.
