Coating comparison (high level)

Use this table for early-stage optioneering only. Final selection belongs to the project corrosion engineer and governing standards.

Topic 3LPE / 3LPP FBE Coal tar / CTE
Primary barrier Thick polyolefin topcoat + adhesive + epoxy primer Thin, dense thermoset epoxy Bituminous / epoxy-bitumen buildups per legacy specs
Typical sweet spot Buried pipelines needing robust mechanical protection Internal/external corrosion control where epoxy is acceptable Older specifications; check HSE restrictions
Key risk to manage Peel adhesion at elevated temps (PP vs PE choice) Handling damage; field repair compatibility Environmental acceptance; worker exposure controls

Footnote: soil chemistry, CP design, operating temperature, and abrasion are decisive - this table is non-exhaustive.
