3LPE / 3LPP external coatings

Three-layer polyolefin systems combine an FBE primer, copolymer adhesive, and polyethylene or polypropylene topcoat for durable external protection on buried and immersed pipelines.

When engineers specify it

Soil stress, groundwater, cathodic protection compatibility, and operating temperature windows drive the choice between PE vs PP top layers.

This page is not a substitute for ISO 21809, DIN, or owner engineering specifications.
