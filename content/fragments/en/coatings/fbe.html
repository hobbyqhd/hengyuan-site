Fusion-bonded epoxy (FBE)

FBE provides a thermoset epoxy barrier on prepared steel. It is widely used as a standalone external layer or as the primer layer in multi-layer systems.

Design considerations

  • Surface profile and cleanliness per SSPC/NACE or ISO equivalents
  • Cure window and intercoat handling for dual-layer FBE
  • Field girth weld FJC systems purchased to match the line pipe coating

Baike references 3PE and FBE among offered corrosion systems - final DFP from the coating applicator governs.
