Products overview

Hongkehua's public profile (Baidu Baike and business registration scope) covers plastic-coated steel pipe, anti-corrosion steel pipe, insulation pipe, hot-dip plastic-lined cable conduit, fittings, and base steel pipe products. Below is a marketing taxonomy aligned to that public information - not a binding material take-off.

Plastic-coated (PE / EP) steel pipe series

Public sources list many variants for water, mining, drainage, and firefighting service - groove, flange, socket, and dual-metal welded joints where applicable.

Water & civil distribution

  • Water-supply coated steel pipe (public listing)
  • External PE / internal EP combinations referenced in Baike
  • Trench, flange, and groove connection styles - per project detail

Mining & firefighting variants

  • Mining dual-resistance coated composite pipe (Baike wording)
  • Firefighting coated steel pipe where UL/FM is not claimed here

TPEP & composite listings

  • TPEP coated pipe cited among Baike products
  • Other composite / plastic-steel combinations per purchaser data sheets

Anti-corrosion steel pipe series

Baike references 2PE/3PE, IPN8710 for potable water, epoxy coal tar enamel, glass-cloth wraps, cement mortar lining, TPEP, epoxy powder, FBE, 2PP/3PP, and similar systems.

Polyolefin & powder systems

  • 2PE / 3PE external systems (line list driven)
  • FBE / dual-layer FBE and epoxy powder (internal or external)
  • 2PP / 3PP where polypropylene is specified

Liquid epoxy & water contact

  • IPN8710 drinking-water epoxy (Baike listing)
  • Liquid epoxy internal lining where invoked by owner specs

Bituminous & cement systems

  • Epoxy coal tar enamel; multi-layer bituminous wraps (Baike)
  • Cement mortar lining for internal corrosion control

Insulation pipe series

Public profile lists PUR foam, steel-jacket steam, buried prefabricated, black/yellow jacket, galvanized-skin aerial, FRP wrap, PERT II, HDPE jacket heating, and related configurations.

District heating & buried network

  • Polyurethane foam insulated steel pipe
  • Pre-insulated buried steel pipe (Baike categories)
  • HDPE / black jacket heating pipe - per thermal design

Steam & elevated routes

  • Steel-jacketed steam insulation pipe
  • Galvanized skin / aerial insulation concepts from Baike

Hot-dip plastic-lined cable conduit

Baike lists hot-dip plastic cable protection tubes, including power cable conduit and N-HAP type names - confirm exact standards with the factory data book.

Power & telecom ducting

  • Hot-dip plastic-lined conduit for cable protection
  • N-HAP conduit naming per Baike - map to project material specs

Pipe fittings & accessories

Registration scope includes elbows, tees, reducers, caps, manholes, expansion joints, supports and hangers, insulating flanges / joints, stainless bends, and related forged / fabricated fittings.

Pressure & piping hardware

  • Flanges, valves (where within scope), prefabricated pipe rack elements
  • Expansion joints and spring / rigid supports (Baike-level listing)

Base steel pipe

Baike notes SSAW (DSAW spiral), HFW / LSAW straight seam, seamless, oil casing, hot-dip galvanized pipe, and hot-expanded pipe as substrates for downstream coating or insulation.

Welded & seamless line pipe

  • Spiral submerged-arc welded pipe
  • LSAW / HFW straight seam; seamless pipe for high pressure classes

OCTG & galvanized

  • Oil casing (Baike listing)
  • Hot-dip galvanized steel pipe where atmospheric corrosion is a driver

Standards & customization

Baike states products can be produced to GB, DIN, ANSI, SY/T, and other standards, with custom design where permitted by equipment and qualifications. Final compliance is always proved by mill and coating documentation, not by this web page.
