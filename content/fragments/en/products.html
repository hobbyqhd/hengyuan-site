Products overview

Hebei Hengyuan product center: plastic-coated steel pipes, 3PE/TPEP anti-corrosion pipes, galvanized pipes, fire-fighting pipes, cable conduits and matching fittings — DN15–DN2200, with customizable colors and joint types subject to contract and datasheets.

Plastic coating series

Internal and external plastic-coated steel pipes for water, firefighting and related services — groove, flange, socket and composite structures as specified.

Highlights

  • Drinking-water and firefighting plastic coating programs
  • Joint systems and steel-plastic layouts per engineering package
  • Hygiene and licensing topics per invoked codes

Anti-corrosion series

3PE/3PP/TPEP and related external systems for buried, immersed and aggressive services — layer build and holiday testing per specification.

Highlights

  • Reinforced and standard grades as defined by the purchaser
  • Combinations with internal epoxy or internal coating as required
  • Spark testing and thickness profiles inside agreed ITP

Steel pipe series

Seamless, spiral and longitudinal welded pipe as substrates for subsequent coating or lining — grades and schedules per mill certificates.

Highlights

  • Spiral welded, LSAW and seamless supply
  • Hot-dip galvanized options where specified
  • Oil-country tubular goods and specials per order

Pipe fittings

Elbows, tees, reducers, caps and flanges aligned with line-pipe coating class and joint design for a complete package.

Highlights

  • Standard and reducing fittings in common schedules
  • Ordered together with coated or lined line pipe
  • GB / ANSI mapping per contract

Standards and customization

Production can be organized to Chinese GB and international references such as DIN, ANSI and SY/T, with customization discussed against written requirements. Binding content is always the contract, technical agreement and mill/coating certificates.
